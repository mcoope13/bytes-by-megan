Why Do I Care About Sir Tim Berners Lee?

I owe at least 40% of the things I've learned and 100% of my career to Sir Tim Berners-Lee. My full time work depends, on the Internet. My freelance work depends on the Internet. The Internet is a far-reaching resource from which I've learned about everything from baseball cards to how to fix the plumbing. Regarded as the creator of the world wide web, without him, I wouldn't have known who he was in the first place, nor would I have been able to indulge my every curiousity and learn more about the world around me, both on the web and away. Thank you, Sir Tim Berners-Lee.
